About the Movement

The Jammu & Kashmir Awami Nationalist Front (JKANF) is a political movement committed to representing the voice, identity, and aspirations of the people.

Our mission is to build unity, promote awareness, and strengthen the collective will of the population.

We believe in discipline, organization, and long-term vision.

History of Kashmir

Kashmir has always been in a conflict since its foundation in 1846 as a princely state. In 1947, the nation became the center of conflict between its two neighbors, India and Pakistan, sparking the First Kashmir War.

Since then, Kashmir has remained divided among different administrations, shaping its political and historical path.

Despite political changes and external pressures, the identity and unity of the people remain central to the region’s future.
