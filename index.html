History

The Jammu & Kashmir Awami Nationalist Front was founded with the objective of representing the political will, identity, and aspirations of the people of Jammu and Kashmir. It emerged during a time when political awareness and public participation were growing across the region.

The movement was created to unite citizens under a common vision of dignity, democratic participation, and national consciousness. From its beginning, the Front emphasized peaceful political organization, public mobilization, and awareness.

The Front continues to grow as more citizens join its mission to strengthen political representation and ensure a stable and respected future for the people.
