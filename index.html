About

The Jammu & Kashmir Awami Nationalist Front (JKANF) is a political movement formed to represent the aspirations, dignity, and collective strength of the people. Our goal is to unite citizens under a shared vision of justice, equality, and national consciousness.

History

The Jammu & Kashmir Awami Nationalist Front was formed as part of a growing realization among the people that a unified political voice was necessary. Over time, the movement gained supporters who believed in political awareness, unity, and organized national effort.

Through grassroots outreach and public engagement, the Front began establishing its presence among the people, building a foundation based on trust, representation, and shared purpose.
